Common questions

Things people ask about pricing.

What counts as a "meal plan"? +
A meal plan is one full plan generation for your chosen number of days (3, 5, 7, or 14 days). Swapping individual meals doesn't count toward your monthly limit — swap as much as you want until you're happy with what's on the menu.
Can I change tiers anytime? +
Yes. Upgrade instantly when you need more. Downgrade at the end of your billing cycle. No fees either way, no retention calls, no hoops to jump through.
What happens after my free trial? +
On day 8, you'll be charged £9.99 and continue on Everyday. Cancel anytime before day 7 and you won't be charged a penny. We'll send you a reminder on day 5 so you've got time to decide.
Do I need an Ocado or Tesco account? +
No. We generate smart search links to Ocado, Tesco, Sainsbury's and Waitrose — use whichever you prefer. Works without any supermarket login required.
Is my data private? +
Your data is stored securely in EU-region servers (Supabase, EU West) and never shared. We don't sell data, we don't run third-party ad trackers. Cancel and your data is deleted within 30 days.
Why is Race Ready a separate add-on? +
Carb loading and race-week planning are specialist features most users only need a few months a year — when they're training for an event. Pricing it as an add-on (or one-off 8-week block) means non-runners don't pay for features they don't use, and runners can drop it after race day.
Can I really cancel anytime? +
Yes — one click in your account settings. No retention calls, no "but wait" pop-ups, no hoops. Your subscription ends at the close of your current billing period.
